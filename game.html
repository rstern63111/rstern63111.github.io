About the Game

Tax Evader's Run is a cyberpunk-themed platformer where you play as an IRS Agent chasing down tax evaders through a neon-lit financial district. Use your agility and special abilities to recover lost revenue and bring tax justice to the city!

Special Abilities

  • Tax Shield - Temporary immunity
  • Audit Strike - Freeze enemies
  • Double Deduction - Extra points

Collectibles

  • Tax Forms - Basic points
  • Golden Deductions - Bonus points
  • Power-up Items - Special abilities

Enemies

  • Tax Evaders - Basic enemies
  • Audit Drones - Flying enemies
  • Paper Storm - Environmental hazards

Controls

  • ←/→ Move left/right
  • SPACE Jump
  • SHIFT Dash
  • Z Special Ability
