Q3 2025 OKRs

Objective 1: Elevate Escalation Quality

  • Increase high-quality escalations to 75%
  • Reduce missing context to 15%
  • Decrease resolution time by 40%

Objective 2: Implement Standard Process

  • 90% template adoption
  • Reduce follow-ups to 1 round
  • 100% automated quality checks

Objective 3: Enhance Knowledge Management

  • Document top 20 escalations
  • Maintain bug documentation
  • 95% bug tracking accuracy

Q4 2025 OKRs

Objective 1: Optimize Resolution Efficiency

  • Simple issues: 2-3 hours
  • Complex issues: <1 business day
  • Bug-related: 2-3 business days

Objective 2: Scale Training Impact

  • 100% training completion
  • 90% assessment scores
  • 50% reduction in common issues

Objective 3: Establish Quality Monitoring

  • Real-time quality scoring
  • 95% SLA compliance
  • Weekly quality reviews
